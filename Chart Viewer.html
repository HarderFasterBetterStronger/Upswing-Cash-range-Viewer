Frequently Asked Questions

What is RFI, vs RFI, Vs 3-Bet Cold, etc? Expand
  • RFI (short for Raise First In): Use these charts when you are the first player to enter the pot preflop.
  • vs RFI: Use these charts when a player in front of you has raised.
  • vs RFI and Call: Use these charts when a player in front of you has raised and another player has called.
  • RFI vs 3-Bet: Use these charts when you are faced with a 3-bet after raising first in.
  • vs 3-Bet Cold: Use these charts when a player in front of you has raised and another player has 3-bet before your action.
  • vs 4-Bet: Use these charts when you are faced with a 4-bet after 3-betting.
  • vs 4-Bet Shove: Use these charts when you are faced with an all-in 4-bet after 3-betting.
  • Blind vs Blind: Use these charts when the action folds to the blinds.
How were these ranges created? Expand

The ranges were created using Monker Solver with 200NL rake.

When I have a 3-betting/4-betting hand, what size do I use? Expand

The suggested raise size is listed above each chart. Some charts show the raise size as a percent of pot, but most show the raise size as the number of small blinds on top of the previous raise.
